Rivian Companion

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Rivian Companion ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

We never see or store your Rivian credentials. Your login information stays on your device only.

Information We Collect

Rivian Companion collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Rivian Account Credentials: Your email and authentication tokens are stored securely on your device to communicate with the Rivian API.
  • Location Data: Saved charging locations (coordinates you provide) and geofence events to enable automatic charging schedule updates.
  • Vehicle Information: Your vehicle ID and name, retrieved from your Rivian account.
  • Charging Preferences: Your saved amperage settings for each location.

How We Use Your Information

All data is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Authenticate with the Rivian API on your behalf
  • Detect when you arrive at a saved charging location
  • Automatically update your vehicle's charging schedule

Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored locally on your device.

Your Rivian credentials and tokens are stored using your device's secure storage mechanisms. We never see or have access to your login information - it exists only on your phone.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates directly with:

  • Rivian API: To authenticate your account and manage your vehicle's charging schedule. This communication is between your device and Rivian's servers only.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Location Permissions

The app requests location permissions to enable geofencing features. Location data is processed locally on your device to detect when you enter a saved charging location. Your location is never sent to us or any server other than what's required by the Rivian API for setting charging schedules.

Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you:

  • Sign out of the app (clears credentials and tokens)
  • Delete a saved location
  • Uninstall the app (removes all data)

Children's Privacy

Rivian Companion is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
